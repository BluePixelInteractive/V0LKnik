ASTRA

L'ASTRA est un conglomérat rassemblant des plus de 300 familles de riches et d'intellectuels sous sa coupe. Ce conglomérat possédait des moyens financiers, humains et logistiques énormes. A la tête du groupe, il avait le chancelier Freeg de Fausty. A son arrivée avec l’ASTRA, tout a changé parce qu’il est venu instaurer un Nouvel Ordre Mondial. Cet ordre mondial qui était selon lui, ce dont la Terre avait besoin pour se sortir de l’état pitoyable dans lequel elle était et par la même occasion, panser ses plaies. STORY

"An aristocrat named Keithy Campbak needs someone to investigate the mysterious silence of the Kera Colony. She asks help from Ueshin Security who rejects the job because of the distance and because she came from Astra. And it’s after that she has no other choice to ask help to Harcos Clan. However, the job turns out to be very different than described." This is how our story starts.
The players will be sent on this planet to investigate and they will discover another story. They will start from a postulate that Nancy is demonic, wants to dominate or revolts for an absurd reason and that it would necessarily have to be destroyed. They will discover over time that this AI is more human than they imagined. The decision will then be whether it must be maintained or must be destroyed. And the players will have to be influenced by the respective experiences of the character they would have chosen.

CHARACTERS

Our characters are not exceptional characters who would have saved the galaxy with beautiful words. We have not imagined super human, just humans as you see every day as you go out of your house. And that's what's cool. Whether it's the PCs or the NPCs, wait for a lambda who has strengths and weaknesses and is fighting his demons or falling into the abyss.
But we paid attention to their personality. We have created unforgettable characters. Our characters will remain in your memory forever because of their way of being, speaking,living.Our characters will be determined by their personality. There will be hypocrites, reserved, extroverts, altruists, etc. But what is interesting is how these multiple personalities react with their environment and with other humans. Will they keep their principles if they have them? Or will they adapt even if they do not take into account what they actually are.
