What is KERA ?

FIRST WORDS

You guys, are the first people out there to go so far in this universe under construction, through its story, and its places, its mysteries. You will also meet the important characters who make up the story and how, directly or indirectly, they write and shape it. We're happy to show you the work we have done so far. Hope you'll enjoy it.

FIRST IDEAS

This project began in may 2015. This was our first try and we decide to make something great and new. We already knew that we aren't going to make a zombie game. Too generic. We are basically passionate gamers who want to make their own game, share something with people like us. But, in the same time, we don't want to imagine and plan something we couldn't achieve. So, we started imagine and write down a little story, an alreday seen world, repetitive and quite boring game mechanics. And we liked that. We were excited.

Now, we added our touches to give to our story something unique after all. And we have finished with something that could attract some players. Our goal was achieved. This was for us the best game in the whole world. Despite this universe already seen by the players, we were theorically ready to jump straight into the production....Wait, this was in 2015. We're now in 2017 and things have changed and we were back with this .
