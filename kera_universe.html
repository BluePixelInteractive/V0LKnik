OUR UNIVERSE

UNIVERSE

We have become a little crazy, in a good way. We burned an old job. Someone should not see it. And the phoenix came out of the ashes. And that's where KERA came out. The universe of KERA has new elements. We took our current solar system as a basis. And we have expanded the universe by creating other systems in the galaxy. These systems in themselves are important to keep the story coherent. This story takes place on the planet Centha IV. More precisely in the human colony of Kera. Before we talk about these places, let's focus on those systems.

THE 5 CERTIFIED SYSTEMS

In this universe, there are five systems that have been certified and recognized as sheltering human life or possibly accommodating humans. Even if some non-human artifacts have already been found on the planets and stars of many of these systems, no contact with a non-human and intelligent life form has yet been made until today. Many people are still believing that human beings will meet another form of life.

The systems are: System One, Baldur Akim system, Vakhadolit system, Zheron Alpha 6 system and the last one is Giltini 69 system. There is a lot of planets, moons and stars with special characteristics and mysteries. We will focus on two for the purpose of the game, but just know that we haven't created all this information just to keep our universe coherent, we will use them in a certain way through books or anothers ways.

Let's talk about our actual system that we call: System ONE.
