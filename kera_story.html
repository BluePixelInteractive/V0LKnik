OUR STORY

STORY

The story begins with Keithy Campbak, an aristocrat living at Neptunia. She needs someone to investigate about the mysterious silence of the Kera Colony.She asks help from Ueshin Security who rejects the job because of the distance and because she works from Astra. And after several failed researches, she has no other choice to ask help to Harcos Clan. However, for the mercenaries sent on Kera colony, the job turned out to be very different than described." This is how our story starts. The players will be sent on this colony to investigate and they will discover another story, another mood. They sent to stop Nancy, the main AI of Kera Colony, they will think that Nancy is demonic,evil and wants to dominate or revolts for an absurd reason and that it would necessarily have to be destroyed. They will discover over time that this AI is more human than they imagined. The decision will then be whether it must be maintained or must be destroyed. And the players will have to be influenced by the respective experiences of the character they would have chosen. And speaking of characters, we had an proper way of how we build those of this story.

CHARACTERS

Our characters are not exceptional characters who would have saved the galaxy with beautiful words. We have not imagined super human, just humans as you see every day as you go out of your house. And that's cool. Whether it's the PCs or the NPCs, wait for a lambda people who has his strengths and his weaknesses. Someone who is fighting against his demons or falling into the abyss.
But we paid attention to their personality. We've created unforgettable characters. These people will stay in your memory forever because of their way of being, speaking,living or thinking.They will be known by their personality. Among them, there will be hypocrites, reserved people, extroverts, altruists, etc. But what is interesting is how those multiple personalities react with their environment and with other humans around them. Will they keep their principles if they have ones ? Or will they adapt even if they do not take into account what they actually are.
