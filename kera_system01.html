SYSTEM ONE

SYTEM ONE

The story begins place in 2632. Centuries before, situation of humans has deteriorated with the lack of resources, wars, growing poverty. The gap between rich and poor people has widened. But there was no apocalypse. No, humanity has evolved, crossed the stars and conquered the planets of the system one: Mars, Jupiter, and so on. Gradually, humans began to leave the earth to discover other locations. Despite this, there still have interestings things happening at Earth, our home.

CITADELS

Citadels are floating megacities found on Earth. They were built and managed by Astra. These megacities welcome only rich people. They were built by rich people , for themselves. All major cities that we know today have disappeared: New York, Vancouver, Cairo, Beijing, Washington, Paris...to let these megacities rise .The most important citadel among them is Neptunia, the citadel of thousand precious stones.

NEPTUNIA

Neptunia is the first citadel built by Astra. An important place in the universe of KERA because it is the representation of the pride and the vanity of the man. This citadel is the main place where important decisions on all systems are made. Magnificent, brilliant, this megacity is seen a thousand miles. For those who are not very rich people and who want to live there, Astra has set unfair and ridiculous rules.
Rules such as prohibition to have sex without prior authorization of a certified judge. Or the retro-debt which stipulates that "if at the age of 65 head of a family has not changed his rank , the family leaves Neptunia and is granted to refund all that the Citadel has offered as services since their installation". We had of course poors & destroyed cities around the globe where people are suffering every day, waiting or calling death due to this rules.

By the way, process of colonization of space has forced humans to create necessary equipment which will serve their ambitions and allow them to sail to infinity.
